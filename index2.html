Basic Infomaion

Common name: parrots

Scientific name: Psittaciformes

Lifespan: an average of 20 to 50 years (varies for each species)

Diet: omnivore; nuts, seeds, flowers, leaves, insects

Regions: in warm climates all over the world; Australia, Central America, South America, Asia
